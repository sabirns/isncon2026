About the Conference

Advancing Nephrology Together

Precision in Renal Care

The 55th Annual Conference of the Indian Society of Nephrology (ISNCON 2026) brings together leading experts, researchers, and healthcare professionals from across the globe to discuss the latest advancements in kidney care and treatment.

This premier medical conference will feature cutting-edge scientific sessions, workshops, and poster presentations covering topics ranging from chronic kidney disease management to novel therapies and transplantation breakthroughs.
